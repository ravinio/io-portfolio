Meet Me

First thing to know about me: I am a nerd and insomniac. What does that mean? Well, when working on my projects such as designing a website or brand identity, I am usually accomplishing this task at 2 am while Lord of the Rings is playing in the background. I am a newly graduated student from Arizona State University and I am eager to begin building experience by taking on freelance projects and searching for other opportunities to work in a team. My design process is mainly focused on identifying a mood and feeling I wish for the user to have and then using the full arsenal of my knowledge, create an experience that will both intrigue and delight the client and the potential user.
